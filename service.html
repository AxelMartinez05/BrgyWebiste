Services of Brgy. Perez


Barangay Profile:

Basic information about the barangay, including history, geography, demographics, and leadership. Contact details of barangay officials.


Announcements and Updates:

Timely dissemination of important announcements, notices, and events related to the barangay. Updates on community projects, programs, and activities.


Online Services:

Online applications for permits, clearances, and other barangay services. Online payment options for fees and taxes.


Community Resources:

Directory of local businesses, institutions, and organizations. Information on healthcare facilities, schools, and other community services. Links to relevant government websites and resources.


Citizen Participation:

Online feedback forms and suggestion boxes for residents to provide input and concerns. Opportunities for online community discussions and forums.


Emergency Response:

Emergency contact numbers and protocols. Information on disaster preparedness and response plans.


Transparency and Accountability:

Publication of barangay budget, expenditures, and financial reports. Access to public documents and records.




Goals:

To enhance communication and transparency between the barangay government and its constituents. To provide convenient and efficient access to barangay services. To foster community engagement and participation. To promote economic development and improve the quality of life of residents.
